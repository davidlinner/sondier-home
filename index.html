Resolve a dispute time- and cost-efficiently

Mediation and arbitration normally cost a lot of effort, time and money. Both parties have great fears of losing their positions and are acting with a lot of strategy.

Our tool is helping both parties to achieve a compromise solution within a few minutes. Just in case remaining positions are too far apart a mediator or arbitrator is suggested.

How does it work?

  • Consensus finding engine

    Parties answer a questionnaire.

  • Consensus basecamp

    Unbiased algorithm helps parties to create a consensus basecamp.

  • Remaining issues

    Parties only have to deal with remaining issues.
